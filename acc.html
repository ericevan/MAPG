Accounting

Gerlaine Ricardo

Gerlaine Ricardo is a certified public accountant who acquired her PhD in taxation from Brown University in 2015. She has worked internationally with clients from China, Japan, Germany and Switzerland, providing them outstanding services in risk assurance, tax returns, transaction and advisory concerns. She is passionate about her field and aims to help build a better working world. Through her work experiences, Gerlaine has successfully applied the use of rhetorical devices, such as kairos, exigence and audience. As a consultant at MAPG, she plans to help clients the proper use of rhetorical devices in order to communicate effectively.

According to Gerlaine, Kairos is used in the Accounting field because “Accountants have to prepare tax returns before the deadline and their reports have to be made at a specific time. Accountants mainly use email as a form of communication with their clients. Through email, they can respond to them quickly and therefore, they are able to provide them exceptional service.”
