Understanding What We Do

Although many different professional fields have similarities regarding to professional writing practices, each field requires a different approach. Let's take the rhetoric device such as Kairos for example. Kairos, a time when conditions are right for the accomplishment of a crucial action. Therefore, if we take Accounting and Mangement, both practice formal writing, but the Kairos are different. For an Accounting, the right condition is when an Accountant have to prepare tax returns before the deadline and they have to be made at a specific time. In that regards, an Accountant is to email and contact whom ever this may concern. Retail Management on the other hand, are to take action when requesting product prices from their vendors. Both professions practices professional writing, but under different conditions.
